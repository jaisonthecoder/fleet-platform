Color

Three layers: brand input → semantic tokens (derived via color-mix) → components. Components never touch raw values. Status colours stay fixed under every brand — expired insurance must look like danger regardless of theme.

Three registers, deliberately

Calm
Booking pages. Drivers need speed — generous whitespace, one clear action, minimal chrome.
Theatrical
The command console. A control room earns density — radar plots, live tickers, dark surfaces.
Cinematic
Executive view. Boardroom screens — large numbers, restrained motion, one story per screen.
