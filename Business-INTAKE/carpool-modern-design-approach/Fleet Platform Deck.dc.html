Architecture

Three rules that cannot be retrofitted

FR-ARC-01
Tenant-ready data model
Every entity carries a tenant key from day one. Phase 1 runs single-tenant; no table assumes it stays that way.
FR-ARC-02
Generic hierarchy engine
N-level configurable tree — deployed as Cluster → Pool → Location. Another org configures Company → Region → Branch.
FR-ARC-03
Rules in configuration, not code
No AD Ports threshold, chain or buffer is ever hard-coded into the system.
Governance

Non-negotiables

Digital consent
No consent, no booking number, no allocation — ever. Bound to driver, vehicle, window and policy version.
Hard blocks
No booking on expired registration or insurance — no override, structurally enforced.
Segregation of duties
8 structural rules — never approve your own booking; Finance and Fleet Manager never co-held on one scope.
AI guardrails
AI flags, humans decide. No AI output autonomously executes a blocking or disciplinary action.
